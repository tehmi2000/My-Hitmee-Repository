About Us

Hitmee is a instant messaging web application that helps you connect with your friends and family all over the globe

How To Login?

To login, just use the phone to your right and chat with our server. Tell it your username and password and it would log you in!

How To Sign Up?

To sign up, you can input a username that you would like to use. If the username is available, our bot would let you know. Alternatively, you can type: /signup, and our bot would direct you on how to sign up.

Made A Mistake?

To restart the chat, just type: /restart. This is known as a bot action. Other actions available are: /signup (To sign up), /back (To go back to the previous question), /about (To learn more about Hitmee)
